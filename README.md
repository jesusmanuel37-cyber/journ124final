## Potential Problems
While looking at the Dataset, it doesn't show the number #1 hits of every week and show every two weeks. This database also only takes to account to the United States listening data and not globally. This, in turn, leads to most of the songs being sung in English and being focused on more Westernized genres. Also, one of the issues with the database is charting the information and making it clearly readable. When categorizing the songs, the database puts multiple labels in one category, and when creating a chart, it makes it difficult for the chart to fully categorize the songs in one group and creates multiple subgroups.
