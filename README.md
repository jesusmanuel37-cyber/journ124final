The data set was collected from Chris Dalla Riva for his book Uncharted Territory and was collected from the Billboard Hot 100 Database.

## Potential Problems
While looking at the Dataset, it doesn't show the number #1 hits of every week, and shows every two weeks. This database also only takes into account the United States listening data and not globally. This, in turn, leads to most of the songs being sung in English and being focused on more Westernized genres. Also, one of the issues with the database is charting the information and making it clearly readable. When categorizing the songs, the database puts multiple labels in one category, and when creating a chart, it makes it difficult for the chart to fully categorize the songs into one group and creates multiple subgroups. 

## Data Analysis

## Ending Summary 
With this Database, we're able to see the trend of music and the most popular formula that most songs use.  Most hit songs on the Billboard Hot 100 are in the genre of Pop and use a BPM of 120.
